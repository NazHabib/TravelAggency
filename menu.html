Featured Places

From various spots that you can visit, we chose the ones we think will blow your minds .

Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island located in the westernmost end of the Lesser Sunda Islands, lying between Java to the west and Lombok to the east. It is one of the country's 33 provinces with the provincial capital at Denpasar towards the south of the island. Bali is the home of the country's largest Hindu population and the largest tourist destination in the world.

Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a country located in South Asia, bordered by India to the north, west, and east, and Myanmar to the southeast. It is the eighth-most populous country in the world, with a population of over 160 million people. Bangladesh is known for its rich culture and history, as well as its vibrant textile and agricultural industries.

Vietnam

Vietnam is a country in Southeast Asia, located on the eastern coast of the Indochinese Peninsula. It is bordered by China to the north, Laos and Cambodia to the west, and the South China Sea to the east. Vietnam is the 13th most populous country in the world, and its capital city is Hanoi. The country is known for its rich culture, beautiful landscapes, and delicious cuisine.
